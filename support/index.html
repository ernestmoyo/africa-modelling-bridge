Ways to support

Three ways in. One movement.

Support can be scoped to the whole movement, a single country, or a single question. Each can be backed on its own or in combination.

Back the movement

The convening core — the engine that makes everything else possible.

  • Bringing ministries and modellers into the same room
  • Country coordination and facilitation
  • Framing the modelling questions each country owns
  • Methods support and supervision for researchers

Good fit for

Capacity-strengthening and research-for-development funders.

Talk to us

Back a country

A full year of the cadence in a single country.

  • Dialogue and discovery sessions
  • A portfolio of country-led modelling questions
  • Researchers placed and supported end-to-end
  • Outputs the ministry can use and cite

Good fit for

Foundations and partners with a country or regional focus.

Talk to us

Back a question

A single modelling question, from framing to finished output.

  • One researcher, one supervisor, one decision
  • A feasible timeline and a clear budget
  • A real answer for a real programme choice
  • A visible result you can point to

Good fit for

Individual donors and mission-aligned philanthropy.

What support enables

Start lean, or go wide.

A first year can be run lean to prove the model in one country, or scaled to run the full cadence across the pilot countries. Indicative figures — final budgets are shaped with the funder.

Lean start

~ USD 25k

Prove the movement end-to-end in one country.

  • ·The full cadence run once, in a single country
  • ·A first set of ministry questions framed and placed
  • ·Researchers supported through to a usable output
  • ·A clean evidence base for a year-two scale-up

Full year

Recommended

~ USD 50k

Run the full cadence across the pilot countries.

  • ·Everything in the lean start, across more countries
  • ·Dialogue and discovery in each pilot country
  • ·A broader portfolio of funded questions
  • ·A working presence in Francophone Africa
