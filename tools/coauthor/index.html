M11 · DOSSIER CO-AUTHOR Coming v1.0

A research supervisor on call.
Drafts the concept note, you keep the judgement.

When a researcher is matched to a dossier, M11 opens a workspace with the dossier on the left and a Claude assistant on the right. The assistant is dossier-aware and discipline-aware — its system prompt encodes the Nick Golding methods register: favour mechanism plus statistics, flag the recurring traps, prefer reproducible workflows.

Ships · v1.0 · month 6 Claude Sonnet · Anthropic API Module · M11

Dossier-aware

Loaded with the dossier context, linked Layer-0 documents and prior session notes. Asks better follow-up questions because it knows what you are working on.

Discipline-aware (the Nick lens)

Favours mechanism plus statistical residual. Flags traps — NegBin replacing the GP, case-only likelihood claiming identifiability. Channels the methods register of a real disease-modelling supervisor.

Concept-note sections

Drafts: background, methods landscape, data sources, expected outputs, ethics statement, timeline, budget justification. You edit; it learns; you ship.

Critique mode

Switch from drafting to critique — paste a section, get a structured review against the working-modeller adviser network's curated standards.

Citations on every turn

Every claim links back to a Layer-0 document, a peer-reviewed paper, or a labelled "this is the model's best guess — verify". No bare assertions.

Editor + adviser checkpoint

Output is always an AI-generated draft. Before any external submission the assigned working-modeller adviser signs off in a 30-minute review.

UNDER THE HOOD · COMING V1.0 · MONTH 6
